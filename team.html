Meet the Team

Noah Burlingame

President and Chairman

Noah Burlingame is the current Chairman and President of Digital Footprint Marketing. He is currently a Junior at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University, Co-Majoring in Marketing and Professional Sales, with a Minor in Music. Noah is currently the Co-Founder and Co-President of the Social Media Marketing Club, as well as the Director of Public Relations for Habitat for Humanity. Outside of academics, Noah plays guitar in bands in both Bloomington and Chicago, and can play other instruments such as the drums, piano, and bass. He is a avid supporter of the Chicago Bears, Cubs, and Blackhawks, and wants to work to Chicago once he graduates.
